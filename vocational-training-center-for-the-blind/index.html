Training Methods

Practical

The training we provide is hands-on and applicable to real-world situations. We teach skills that can be used in everyday life such as navigating public spaces, using assistive technology and communicating effectively.

Based on Individual Needs

No two individuals have the same training needs so we get to know each individual and then tailor our training to their unique needs, ensuring that everyone receives the support they need to reach their goals.

Results Oriented

We believe that the ultimate goal of our training is to help people achieve their goals and live their best lives. We track progress and measure success, ensuring that our training methods are delivering the desired results.

Our Courses

Typewriting

Our typewriting course helps our trainees get acquainted with typing with little or no aid before moving to a computer system.

Crafts

We offer training in crafts such as bead, bag and shoe making, tie and dye (adire), cane weaving
